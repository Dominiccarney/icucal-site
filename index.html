ICUcal

ICU physician shift scheduling and invoice management for macOS.

What it does

ICUcal is a native Mac app that helps ICU departments manage physician shift schedules. It syncs with Google Calendar to keep schedules up to date and generates PDF invoices and reports for billing.

Features

  • Google Calendar sync — Push and pull shift schedules directly from your calendar.
  • Multi-unit support — Manage multiple ICU departments, each with its own roster, shift lines, and rates.
  • Invoice generation — Export monthly invoice packages as PDF with detailed hour breakdowns.
  • Shift balance tracking — Year-to-date reports with FTE calculations.
  • Holiday coverage history — Five-year lookback for Christmas and New Year's shifts.
